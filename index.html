Intent is not a feature — it's a philosophy. We believe that every design decision should trace back to a human need, a behavioural truth, or a strategic goal.

We don't design surfaces. We design systems of meaning — where every interaction, every transition, every word has been placed with deliberate purpose.

Intent-led experiences are felt before they're understood. They guide without instructing, delight without distracting, and resolve without effort.

Three minds,
one intent.

Studio3.design was founded on a belief that has guided every project since: design without intent is decoration.

We are three designers who met at the intersection of craft and cognition — where the way something works is inseparable from the way it feels. We've spent our careers studying human behaviour, building AI systems, and crafting interfaces that dissolve the distance between a person and their goal.

Our practice centres on intent-led experience design — a methodology that starts not with wireframes or user stories, but with a deep excavation of what people actually want to accomplish, and why. We map the gap between stated and latent intent, then design experiences that bridge it.

We work with early-stage startups, scaling platforms, and enterprise teams who believe that the quality of the experience is the quality of the product.
